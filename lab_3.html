Campus Life

During my time at university, I had the chance to dive into a range of programs and activities that really shaped my personal and academic journey. One of the highlights was attending the UNI-Versal Symposium: Empower Your Mind, where I listened to some incredible speakers like Mr. Iddin Ramli, Madam Najwa Latif, and Mr. Sakthivel. Their insights truly inspired me and pushed me to always seek personal growth.


Beyond academics, I took on a leadership role with the Exco Corporate and Network Members, which was a fantastic opportunity to hone my communication and teamwork skills. It also gave me the chance to connect with fellow student leaders and learn about the ins and outs of managing student organizations.


Volunteering was another rewarding part of my campus experience. I got involved in the Boost Learning Program for SPM Students at PPTA UTHM, where I guided students through the university library and showed them all the resources available. It felt great to support and motivate younger students on their academic paths.


When I wasn't busy with formal events, I loved hanging out with friends, whether we were studying together or just chilling around campus. These moments not only enriched my university experience but also helped me grow as a person, both personally and professionally.
